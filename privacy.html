We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for third-party advertising.

Where the GDPR applies, we process personal data on the basis of: your consent (when you submit the form), performance of a contract (delivering our services), legitimate interests (site security, responding to business enquiries), and legal obligation (record keeping).
