Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This policy explains how cancellations and refunds work for services purchased from astra-meta LLC. It forms part of our Terms of Service.

1. Monthly service plans

  • Monthly plans are billed in advance for each service period and renew automatically.
  • You may cancel at any time. To stop the next renewal, notify us at least 7 days before your renewal date.
  • After cancellation, services continue until the end of the period you already paid for.
  • Because work (strategy, campaign setup, automation build) begins at the start of each period, fees for a period that has already started are generally non-refundable.
  • If we have not yet started work for a newly started period, contact us — we will review the request and may issue a full or partial refund at our discretion.

2. One-time Consultation & Problem Analysis Session

  • Full refund if you cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before the scheduled session.
  • Once the session has taken place and the analysis has been delivered, the fee is non-refundable, as the service has been fully performed.
  • If we cancel or cannot deliver the session, you receive a full refund or a rescheduled session — your choice.

3. Advertising spend

Advertising budget is not part of our fees. Money you spend on Meta, Google, TikTok or other platforms is paid by you directly to those platforms. We cannot refund advertising spend, and unused platform balances must be claimed from the platform itself.

4. Service issues

If you are unhappy with the service, contact us first. We will work with you to fix the issue, redo the work, or — where we have clearly failed to deliver what was agreed — agree a fair refund of the affected portion of the fee. Because outcomes of advertising depend on factors outside our control, refunds are not available simply because results did not meet expectations (see clause 5 of our Terms of Service).

5. How to cancel or request a refund

Email aydin@astra-meta.com from the address associated with your account, including:

  • Your name and company name
  • The plan or service purchased
  • The date of purchase
  • Whether you are cancelling, or requesting a refund and why

We acknowledge every request within 2 business days.

6. How refunds are issued

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method, normally within 5–10 business days of approval. Your bank or card issuer may need additional time to post the credit. Refunds are made in the original transaction currency; we are not responsible for exchange-rate differences or bank fees.

7. Chargebacks

Please contact us before opening a dispute with your bank or card issuer. Almost every issue can be resolved directly and far faster. We are happy to provide records of the services delivered if a dispute is raised.
