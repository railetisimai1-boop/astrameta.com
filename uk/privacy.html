Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Who we are

Astra Meta LLC ("we", "us") provides revenue-recovery automation services to businesses. You can reach us at info@astra-meta.com.

Business contact data

We process publicly available business contact information — company names, business email addresses and business phone numbers — in order to send relevant business-to-business communications. We rely on legitimate interest as the lawful basis (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(f)) and, for corporate subscribers, Regulation 22 of PECR.

Your choices

Every email we send includes a working opt-out. Reply "no" to any of our emails, or write to info@astra-meta.com, and we will remove your details within 48 hours and suppress any future contact.

Data we hold

Company name, business contact details and correspondence history. We do not sell personal data. We retain minimal suppression records solely to honour opt-out requests.

Client data

When clients connect systems to us for a loss audit, access is read-only, limited in scope, and revocable at any time. Audit data is deleted on request.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting info@astra-meta.com. If you are in the UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).
